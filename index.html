Welcome to Grace's Bakes! My name is Grace, and I'm a home baker based in Swindon.

This business blossomed from a loving hobby for baking and arts & crafts. My original focus was making cupcakes and treats for my family and friends... then I began to experiment with different flavours and diversified into layered cakes!

My decorating skills grew from there and, with the help of family and friends, Grace's Bakes was born.

  • Registered with the Swindon Borough Council
  • Completed 'Level 2 Food Hygiene and Safety for Catering'
  • Completed 'Food Allergen Awareness' online course
  • Received a Level 2 Distinction in GCSE Level 1/2 Vocational Award Hospitality and Catering
